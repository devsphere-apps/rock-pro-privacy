Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Rock Identifier Pro ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • Display name (optional, for personalization)
  • Profile picture (optional, stored securely)

Usage Data

  • Photos you upload for rock identification
  • AI analysis results and rock collection data
  • App usage statistics and feature interactions
  • Device information (OS version, app version, device model)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide rock identification and analysis services
  • Maintain your personal rock collection
  • Improve our AI models and app functionality
  • Send important app updates and security notifications
  • Process subscription payments and manage premium features
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Images are stored securely using industry-standard cloud storage
  • Account data is protected with secure authentication
  • We use Supabase for secure database management
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • In connection with a business merger or acquisition (users will be notified)

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your rock collection data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

To delete your account, visit our account deletion page at: https://devsphere-apps.github.io/account-deletion-page/ or use the "Delete Account" option in the app settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Rock collection data is kept until you delete it or close your account
  • Usage analytics are retained for up to 2 years
  • Deleted data is permanently removed within 30 days

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: devsphere.apps@gmail

We are committed to resolving any privacy concerns promptly and transparently.
